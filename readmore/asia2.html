Couples Vacation in Phuket + Pha Nga Bay (James Bond Island)


Come relax on the beaches of Bangtao in Phuket, Thailand. Then jump on a boat to explore Phang Nga Bay and James Bond Island!

Skip the early morning crowds and take in a beautiful ocean sunset on this island-hopping boat trip around Phang Nga Bay from Phuket. Offering an exclusive experience for no more than 25 travelers, you’ll enjoy a relaxed cruise to Khao Tapu—AKA James Bond Island due to its starring role in The Man With The Golden Gun.
