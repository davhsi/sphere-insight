Turns out I’m FINNISH! And we’re exploring Helsinki


Turns out I’m part Finnish! So I decided to go explore Helsinki, Finland. It’s my first time in this Nordic country so let’s see what we get up to!

Helsinki is the capital of Finland and a beautiful city with a mix of old and new. There are many things to see and do in Helsinki, including visiting the Suomenlinna Sea Fortress, exploring the Design District, and taking a walk through the Esplanade Park.
